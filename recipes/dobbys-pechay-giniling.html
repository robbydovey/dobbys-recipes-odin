Dobby's Pechay Giniling

Description

A recipe that is most beloved in the Bat-og-Angeles household. This has been a frequently-cooked dish as it evokes memories from Dobby's elementary days, when he first made this dish from home-grown Pechay plants

Ingredients

  • 3-4 bundles of Pechay plants
  • 1/4kg ground pork
  • 1 piece red onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooking Oil
  • Maggi Magic Sarap

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients for stir-fry by mincing the onions and garlic. Slice the Pechay plants crosswise to create the noodly shape of the leaves.
  2. Sautee the minced garlic and onions.
  3. Once the garlic is golden brown, add the ground pork. Stir-fry until the ground pork is cooked (white-golden brown appearance.)
  4. Add soy sauce and stir-fry until the soy sauce has seeped into the meat.
  5. Add water until meat is submerged. Add salt, pepper, and Magic Sarap to taste.
  6. Once preferred taste has been achieved, add the sliced Pechay leaves.
  7. Let dish boil until the Pechay leaves are cooked. Adjust by adding salt or pepper to taste.
