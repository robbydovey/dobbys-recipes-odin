Mimits' Sinigang

Description

A dish made by Mimits that is a favorite by Dobby. Perfect for cold weather and as a weekend ulam.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg sinigang-cut pork (more fat, the better)
  • 2 bundles of Kangkong leaves
  • 1 piece gabi
  • 1 piece tomato
  • 2 pieces red onion
  • 2 pieces green chilis
  • Sinigang Mix
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fish Sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients by quartering the onions. Slice the Gabi into small, bite-size pieces.
  2. Boil the meat in a pot with some pepper to your preferred softness (typically around 30 minutes)
  3. Separate the meat from the soup
  4. In a separate pan, stir-fry the onions and tomatoes together.
  5. Add the meat into the mix. Add a bit of fish sauce to taste. Stir-fry until the surface of the meat is seared.
  6. Add stir-fried mixture back into the soup.
  7. Add sliced gabi. Boil until gabi is tender
  8. Add entire contents of Sinigang Mix. Cover the pot and let simmer.
  9. After a few minutes, add salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Add kangkong leaves and green chili.
  11. Adjust to taste by adding salt, pepper, and sinigang mix.
